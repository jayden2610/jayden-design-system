We make things
move.

Founded in 2020, Otomo is a studio built around the belief that motion is the most direct way to make people feel something. We design identities that live in time — brand films, spatial installations, digital experiences, and printed matter that refuses to sit still.

Our work has been recognised by ADC Awards, D&AD, and the Tokyo Type Directors Club. We collaborate with cultural institutions, independent brands, and people who care about craft.

Motion

Brand films, typographic animation, title sequences, explainers, and generative motion systems.

Brand

Identity design, visual language, art direction, and brand guidelines for culture-driven clients.

Spatial

Exhibition design, immersive environments, installation direction, and spatial branding.

Digital

Interactive experiences, website design, prototyping, and digital brand presences.
