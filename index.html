Glass Layer

.glass

Default restraint — 20px blur, subtle border. For most floating elements.

.glass-deep

52px blur, topmost floating element only. Deeper blur = higher z-layer.

.glass-dense

Higher opacity for reliable text contrast ≥ 4.5:1. For content-heavy overlays.
